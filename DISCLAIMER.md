## Crisis Situations

If you or someone you know is in crisis, experiencing suicidal ideation, or in immediate danger, **contact emergency services (911 in the US) or a crisis line immediately**:

- **988 Suicide and Crisis Lifeline:** Call or text **988** (US)
- **Crisis Text Line:** Text **HOME** to **741741** (US)
- **Emergency services:** **911** (US) or your local emergency number

Do not rely on this suite or any AI tool in a crisis. These resources are staffed by trained professionals who can help.
