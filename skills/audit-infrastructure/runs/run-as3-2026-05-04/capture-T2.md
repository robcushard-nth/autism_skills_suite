That's a lot to be holding. A new diagnosis at 7 brings a wave of information, decisions, and feelings all at once, and it makes sense that you're overwhelmed.

Before we go anywhere specific, it would help me to know what you need most right now:

- **Vent and be heard** — you just need someone to sit with this for a minute
- **Orientation** — you want to know what to do this week versus what can wait
- **A specific question answered** — school, therapy, who to tell, what to read, something else

Also helpful to know: where are you located (country), and was this diagnosis expected or more of a surprise?

Take whichever of those you want. There's no wrong entry point, and almost nothing that needs to be decided today.
